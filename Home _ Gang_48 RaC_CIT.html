    Welcome to

    ROTARACT CLUB OF COIMBATORE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

    RaC_CIT

    Carrying the legacy of being one of the oldest clubs, Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Institute of Technology well known as RaC CIT was evoked in the year 1977 with the help of Rtn. P. R. Ramakrishnan, Founder of Coimbatore Institute of Technology and District Governor(1997) with the motto “Self development and Fellowship through Service”. The Club was chartered on 14th January 1977 with Charter President Rtr. PP. Shyam Sundar supported by its Parent Club, Rotary Club of Coimbatore East.

    About

    LOGO DESCRIPTION

    A ship's helm, rising above, symbolizes our commitment to action and service that traverses uncharted waters with purpose. Six sturdy handles encircle the helm, representing the core pillars of our endeavor – Club, Community, Professional International, Rotary Foundation, and District Priority projects. These handles fortify our ship, uniting us in our collective pursuit of positive change. In the helm's center, the Rotary wheel logo shines like the sun, reminding us that our actions radiate wherever light reaches. Two gentle waves cradle our theme, "SEYAL," a Tamil word for sustainable action, signifying the boundless reach of our impact, much like water connecting distant shores. Beneath the waves, an anchor holds steadfast, symbolizing the strength of our legacy. Inscribed with 'Gang 48,' it marks us as the 48th Rotaract Board of our institution. This anchor underscores our belief that every small initiative has the potential to create a monumental impact. Together, this emblem represents our unwavering commitment to navigating the seas of service, driven by the power of unity, sustainability, and legacy, ensuring our actions leave an enduring mark wherever they go.
